Why Spaghetti Blockchain?

Spaghetti Blockchain delivers innovative digital healthcare blockchain solutions to healthcare payers and providers to manage clinical trials data and electronic medical records while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Data Security 100%
Transparency 100%
Management Efficiency 100%

Our Service Area

With the advantages of Spaghetti Blockchain, it offers hospital, clinic, labs and insurers a secured low cost medical records management solution.

Hospitals

A full management tool is provided to doctors in hospitals to view and add comments to in and out patients' health records.

Clinics

Private doctors can access and view patients's health records after getting the approval. They can have more understanding about the patients.

Patients

Patients can view their own health status and records everywhere and anytime. It is easier for patient to manage their records.

Insurers

A more convenient way for insurers to communicate with the insured and review their health status and medical records.

Labs

Laboratories can use the patients' medical records for research purpose after getting approval from the patients through our system.

Pharmacies

Similar to the Laboratories, pharmaceutical companies can research and develop more drugs with the patients' medical data.
